GirlCode

We are committed to helping you discover nearby clubs and organizations that empower girls with opportunities to learn, grow, and excel in coding. Our goal is to connect you with spaces that foster a supportive and inclusive environment, enabling young girls to develop their coding skills and thrive in the world of technology. Together, we can create a community that promotes diversity, equality, and a passion for STEM education among girls. Let's embark on this journey of discovery and empowerment together!
